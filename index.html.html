The Artist

Jason Noah

Jason Noah is a multidisciplinary artist whose work bridges nature, memory, and human emotion. Working across canvas and organic surfaces such as real tree leaves, he transforms fragile materials into lasting visual stories.

"Even a single leaf can hold a universe of emotion."

His art is deeply influenced by his personal journey and cultural roots, often reflecting themes of survival, identity, and connection. Each piece carries a sense of movement and life, where texture and detail invite the viewer into a quiet but powerful dialogue.

Every painting begins not with a plan, but with a feeling. A gesture, a colour, a memory that quietly insists on being expressed. Jason follows that instinct until the work finds its own voice, often revealing a truth he did not know he was carrying. The result is art that feels both deeply personal and universally human.

He has exhibited his work across many countries, bringing his unique vision to international audiences who find in his work both the familiar and the profoundly unexpected.
